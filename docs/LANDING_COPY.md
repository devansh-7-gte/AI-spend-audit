### Conversion Optimization Notes

- **Headline**: "Instantly" creates urgency (beats "quickly")
- **CTA**: "Free audit" beats "sign up"
- **Social Proof**: Specific numbers ($2,400) beat generic praise
- **Pain Point**: "No manual work" appeals to busy professionals
- **Differentiation**: "30 seconds" vs competitors' longer processes

### A/B Testing Priorities

1. Headline: "Instantly" vs "Get results in 30 seconds"
2. CTA: "Start free" vs "Claim your free audit"
3. Social Proof: Numbers vs testimonial quotes
4. Offer: Free forever vs time-limited free trial
