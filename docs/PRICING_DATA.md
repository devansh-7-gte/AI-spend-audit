# Pricing Data

## Pricing Model

### Freemium Model (Proposed)

#### Free Tier
- 5 audits per month
- Basic analysis (totals, categories)
- No historical tracking
- No export functionality
- Features: Category breakdown, trend detection

#### Pro Tier ($9.99/month)
- Unlimited audits
- Advanced analytics
- 12-month history
- Export (CSV, PDF)
- Custom categories
- Email reports
- Features: All free features + recommendations, trend analysis

#### Business Tier ($29.99/month)
- Everything in Pro
- Team collaboration (up to 5 users)
- API access
- Custom integrations
- Priority support
- Advanced reporting

### Pricing Justification

- **Free**: Drives user acquisition, builds habit
- **Pro**: Captures serious personal finance users
- **Business**: Targets small teams and accountants

## Customer Segments

### Primary
- Individuals tracking personal spending ($9.99 TAM)
- Small business owners ($29.99 TAM)

### Secondary
- Financial advisors (white-label opportunity)
- Accounting firms

## Revenue Projections

### Conservative Scenario (Year 1)
- 1,000 free users
- 50 Pro subscribers @ $9.99 = $5,000/month
- 10 Business subscribers @ $29.99 = $3,000/month
- **Total**: $8,000/month = $96,000/year

### Optimistic Scenario (Year 1)
- 10,000 free users
- 500 Pro subscribers @ $9.99 = $50,000/month
- 100 Business subscribers @ $29.99 = $30,000/month
- **Total**: $80,000/month = $960,000/year
